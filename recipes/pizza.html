Pizza

Description

This is a recipe for a classic plain pizza. Pizza's have proven themselves through their timeless popularity around the globe to be one of the worlds best items of food.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Lay out the pizza dough in a circular shape
  2. Apply tommato sauce to the dough
  3. Add the cheese
  4. Cook in oven
  5. Congragts! Your done!
