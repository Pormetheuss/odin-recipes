Stuffed Grape Leaves

Description

Stuffed Grape Leaves are a delicasy every human mouth should have the pleasure of consuming.

Ingredients

  • Grape leaves
  • Rice
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Roll out grape leaves
  2. Stuff with rice and meat
  3. Roll grape leaves into cylindrical shape
  4. Cook
  5. Phenomenal! You now have one of the worlds greatest foods ready to be consumed!
